# 手握玫瑰的理性

## 声明

本文描述的是一种认知态度,而非哲学理论体系

## 宣言

人类作为生物,天生具有对于未知的恐惧,这种倾向会体现于环境,信息上

然而我认为理性的态度而非去消除恐惧,而是承认恐惧存在,随着认知对于知识和信息的探索,未知可能会在信息变得更加广阔的情况下,未知的恐惧可能不会缓解,甚至会加深

但是理性的态度是尽可能追求严谨和真实,而不满足于那些仅仅能缓解对未知的焦虑,却缺乏真实和充分依据的**廉价答案**之上

**廉价答案:** 指主要服务于心理安慰，而非建立在充分理由之上的解释

## 澄清

追求理性的态度,并不是说无休止无底线地去探索,人类的认知和现实资源是有限的,不仅限于**廉价答案**之上,也需要在认知无法承受的情况下适可而止的停留

## 对于标题的解释

将理性比作一位手握玫瑰的骑士,玫瑰的美,在于它值得守护.尖刺的存在.则意味着守护并非没有代价.未知会刺伤人.怀疑会动摇人.求真甚至可能增加痛苦,然而理性的尊严恰恰在于: 它不是因为没有恐惧才继续前行,而是在承认恐惧之后,仍然拒绝以虚假的确定性换取心安

# Rationality with a Rose in Hand

## Statement

This text describes a cognitive attitude, not a philosophical theoretical system.

## Manifesto

As biological beings, humans possess an innate fear of the unknown; this tendency manifests in environments and information.

However, I believe that a rational attitude is not to eliminate fear, but to acknowledge its existence. As cognition explores knowledge and information, and as information becomes vaster, the fear of the unknown may not alleviate, and may even deepen.

But a rational attitude means pursuing rigor and truth as much as possible, rather than being satisfied with **cheap answers** that merely alleviate anxiety about the unknown yet lack truth and sufficient evidence.

**Cheap Answers:** Refers to explanations that primarily serve psychological comfort rather than being built upon sufficient reasons.

## Clarification

Pursuing a rational attitude does not mean exploring endlessly and without boundaries; human cognition and practical resources are limited. One should not only avoid limiting oneself to **cheap answers**, but also need to stop and stay within reasonable limits when cognition becomes unbearable.

## Explanation of the Title

Rationality is compared to a knight holding a rose. The beauty of the rose lies in the fact that it is worth protecting. The existence of thorns implies that protection does not come without a cost. The unknown will sting people, doubt will shake people, and the pursuit of truth may even increase pain. However, the dignity of rationality lies precisely in this: it does not move forward because it is free of fear, but because after acknowledging fear, it still refuses to exchange peace of mind for false certainty.
